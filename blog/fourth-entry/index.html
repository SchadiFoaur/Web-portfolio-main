Blog page designing - Forth Entry

January 10, 2022

Although it took around 3 days. I am now officially done with the homepage. I made some minor adjustments, color corrections, and some simple css animations. After i was finished with the home page i moved to the blog page. Since i had already been familiar with everything, it didn't take much time to redesign the entire page. I made it pretty close in concept to the homepage theme. I also added an icons near the titles and linked one of them to the very first entry i wrote.
Then i decided to make an image carousal and thought it would be better to show before/after images. I tried about 6 times, but was unsuccessful. My guess is that whatever i am doing wrong is messing with bootstrap's javaScript and ruining the entire website.
After i gave up i added the footer to all my previous entries and naturally a new problem came up. The footer was not sticking to the bottom of the page but to the last line of the content. For example if all the content took only 50% of the page then the footer would be sticking directly under the content and not the bottom of the entire page. I did all i could, but was again unsuccessful, so i just decided to add more content to the page. that way it would be forced to be at the very bottom.
