Redesigning everything - Second Entry

January 8, 2022

I was not successful in removing the borders of the Gif, so i just decided to blend it with background. I went with a dark theme because it looked cleaner and better. I then decided to change the entire layout of my homepage after some research on Bootstrap's Grid layout . I decided on something simple.
The animated Gif would be on the left and the introductory on the right. So i rounded the corners of the Gif and made like an avatar/profile. It was fairly easy and simple. Then suddenly the first problem of the day came up. The avatar and the content were too close to each other. It was easy separating them from each other. After separating the content two new problems came up. The contents are now sticking closely to the "Nav Bar" and the avatar is not responsive enough. I squeezed my screen and realized that the avatar was getting squeezed parallel to the screen instead of getting smaller.
I will spend the entire night learning more about responsiveness and box model.
