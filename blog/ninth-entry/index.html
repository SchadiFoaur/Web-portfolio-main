New Blog and Nav - Ninth Entry

April, 2022

After I was done with my home page, I moved to the Blog page. However before moving the nav bar had to be created. I created the nav bar normally and added four sections (navigation buttons). The first one is the home that takes you to the home page, the second is the Blog that takes you to the main Blog page, the third was About that takes you to the About section that is directly under my picture in the home page and lastly the contact which takes you to the contact form where you would fill out a form and send it to me.
After coding the nav bar, I worked on making it responsive for example when on mobile devices the nav bar turns into a hamburger bar when clicked the entire nav comes to screen.
Lastly I worked on the main blog page, I decided to go with a card design and then I sorted them out old and new. If the old button is pressed, only the entries that discussing the old website will show. If new is pressed, only the entries discussing the new will show.
